Tiger 1 Corp.

Expanding access to sports training and mentorship for underserved youth in the Atlanta area.

Our Mission

Tiger 1 Corp. is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing youth in the Atlanta area with access to sports training, mentorship, and safe athletic development—regardless of financial circumstances. While we started with baseball, we aim to offer basketball, football, and other sports opportunities as we grow. Our goal is to empower youth, build confidence, and foster teamwork through athletics.

Our Vision

Our long-term vision is to establish a community-based indoor sports facility that provides year-round access to baseball, basketball, football, and other athletic programs. This space will serve as a safe, structured environment for skill development, mentorship, and life skills.

Why We Started

Tiger 1 Corp. was founded to address the lack of accessible athletic programs for underserved youth in the Atlanta area. We believe every child deserves a place to learn, grow, and play, regardless of their family’s financial situation.

Programs (Coming Soon)

  • Pop-up baseball, basketball, and football clinics
  • Youth mentorship through sports
  • Access to equipment for families in need
  • Community partnerships with schools and local organizations

Tiger 1 Corp. is currently in its early development stage and actively working to build programs that meet the needs of our community.
